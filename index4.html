Controlled Social Media

Facebook built what is the current social media platform but also the most restrictive when it come to visual culture and individuality. Facebook controls the aesthetics of the page and the users only flexibility to be unique is the profile, cover photo and what the user decides to post. Which visually is very limiting but Facebook created something that could be seen as more individual, by allowing posts it meant you could share you voice to more people on you interests and opinions. Facebook took the users strength way when in came to the users individual visual style but gave them strength by adding news feeds and not just a personal profile.
